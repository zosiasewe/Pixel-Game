# Pixel Advent Calendar Game

A cozy pixel art platformer that unfolds throughout December, revealing new animals and magical worlds each day as an interactive advent calendar experience!!!

This game was created for fun as a festive advent calendar experience

## My pixel-art
- **Animals**
- **Player**
- **Small assets**

## Features

###  Time-Gated Content
- **Daily Unlocks**: New content appears each day throughout December
- **December 1+**: World 1 and first outfits unlock
- **December 7+**: World 2 and additional outfit unlock
- **December 11+**: World 3 becomes accessible
- ...
- Animals appear progressively as days pass

###  Multiple Biomes
Explore three distinct worlds, each with its own unique biome and atmosphere:
- **World 1**: Starting area with friendly creatures
- **World 2**: New environment with different animals
- **World 3**: Special unlockable world (requires collecting snacks)
- ...

###  Wardrobe System
Collect magical potions to unlock new character appearances:
- Collect 6 hearts to unlock Outfit 2
- Collect 6 leeks to unlock Outfit 3
- Collect 6 shakes to unlock Outfit 4
- ...
- Switch between unlocked outfits anytime with number keys (1-4)

### Gameplay Elements
- Smooth platforming mechanics
- Moving platforms to navigate
- Drop-through platforms for vertical exploration
- Character transformations that change your appearance

##  Controls

### Movement
- **Arrow Keys**: Move left and right
- **Space**: Jump
- **Down Arrow**: Drop through platforms

### World Travel
- **Q**: Travel to World 1
- **W**: Travel to World 2
- **E**: Travel to World 3
- ...

### Outfit Switching
- **1**: Default outfit
- **2**: Outfit 2 (unlock with hearts)
- **3**: Outfit 3 (unlock with carrots)
- **4**: Outfit 4 (unlock with shakes)
- ...

##  Technical Details

**Engine**: Godot 4

**Key Game Features**:
- Date-based content unlocking system
- Persistent outfit and world progression
- Dynamic animation system with outfit variants
- Collectible tracking and reward spawning
